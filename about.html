Our Website was created in order to teach people, specifically students about Fake News as the give information in regards to real news which is going on around the world. This website was created in 2020 with the aim of making sure that people are aware that fake news is a problem in society and by learning about fake news and where it is likely to appear, it can help stop misinformation from spreading which can also lead to lives being saved. This website is a non-profit, we do not have Advertisement or make money off of traffic like other websites do, which is a reason why we are even more motivated to ensure that you get the best quality news and education on fake news through the videos on the site and information in the Latest News section.
